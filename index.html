Going To The Beach Information

Chart Statistics Explained

The bar graph nearby shows all important factors to know when deciding to go to the beach. Such as: an average of how rainy it is, how cloudy it is, how sunny it is, your chances of getting hit by a truck while lying on the sand, and how likely it is that your phone will die while having a good time!

One very very VERY important statistic to factor in when going to the beach is how flipping much it snows! You could be lying there on the beach in 105 degree weather, getting a fantastic tan, and out of no where it is an absolute blizzard outside! I'm talking 2ft of snow in just 2 minutes, frozen solid oceans, and everyone running frantically so the snow doesn't pile up on top and smother them to death! Other than all of this...the beach is wonderful and miraculous! I recommend 10/10!
